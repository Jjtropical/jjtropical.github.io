About University District Donuts

Welcome to University District Donuts, where every bite is a sweet delight! Our cozy corner in the heart of the university district is a haven for donut enthusiasts craving mouthwatering flavors and irresistible treats. From classic glazed to inventive creations like maple bacon and blueberry old fashioned, our menu offers a delectable assortment to satisfy every palate. Dive into our world of delectable indulgence and experience the joy of freshly baked goodness with each bite. Whether you're a student in need of a study break or a local craving a delightful treat, University District Donuts is your go-to destination for a taste of happiness.
